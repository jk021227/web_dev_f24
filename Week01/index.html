Description

Bibimbap is a Korean dish that is made with rice, vegetables, and meat.
It is a popular dish in Korea and is often served in a hot stone bowl.
The dish is typically topped with a fried egg and gochujang, a spicy Korean sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked white rice
  • 1/2 pound of sliced beef (ribeye or ground beef)
  • 1 zucchini, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 fried egg (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice and set it aside to keep warm.
  2. Stir-fry the beef in a pan with soy sauce until fully cooked.
  3. SautÃ© the vegetables (zucchini, carrot, spinach, and bean sprouts) separately with a little sesame oil and salt.
  4. In a bowl, place the rice at the bottom, then arrange the cooked beef and vegetables on top.
  5. Add the fried egg on top (optional).
  6. Drizzle with sesame oil and gochujang.
  7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve immediately.
